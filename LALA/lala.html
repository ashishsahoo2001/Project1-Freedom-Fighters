Lala Lajpat Rai

Indian Independence Activist

Born: January 28, 1865

Martyred: November 17, 1928

Rai was a strong advocate for swaraj (self-rule) and played a significant role in the Non-Cooperation Movement.

Early Life

Rai was born in Dhudike, Punjab, India.

He was a lawyer and a nationalist.

Freedom Movement

Rai was a key figure in the Indian independence movement.

He founded the Servants of the People Society and was imprisoned several times for his activism.
