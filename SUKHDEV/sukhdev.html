Sukhdev

Indian Revolutionary

Born: May 15, 1907

Martyred: March 23, 1931

Sukhdev was a prominent figure in the Indian independence movement.

Early Life

Sukhdev was born in Ludhiana, Punjab, India.

He was a close associate of Bhagat Singh and Shivaram Rajguru.

Freedom Movement

Sukhdev was involved in the Hindustan Republican Association and participated in the Lahore Conspiracy Case.

He was executed by the British for his revolutionary activities.
