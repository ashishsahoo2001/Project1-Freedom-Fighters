Aruna Asaf Ali

Indian Independence Activist

Born: July 16, 1909

Died: July 29, 1996

Aruna was a leading figure in the Indian independence movement and a renowned socialist.

Early Life

Aruna was born in Kalka, Punjab, India.

She was educated in India and England.

Freedom Movement

Aruna was a key figure in the Quit India Movement.

She hoisted the Indian National Flag at the Gowalia Tank maidan in Bombay in 1942.
