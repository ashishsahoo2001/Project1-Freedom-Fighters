Early Life

Baji Rout was born on October 5, 1926, in Nilakanthaipur, Odisha, India.

He was the youngest of four siblings.

Baji Rout

Indian Freedom Fighter

Born: October 5, 1926

Died: December 11, 1938

Baji Rout was a young Indian freedom fighter who sacrificed his life at the age of 12 during the Indian independence movement.

Freedom Movement

Baji Rout joined the Indian freedom movement at a young age.

He was inspired by Mahatma Gandhi's principles of non-violent resistance.

He participated in various protests and marches against British rule.

Legacy

Baji Rout's sacrifice and bravery inspired many young Indians to join the freedom movement.

He is remembered as a hero and a symbol of courage in the face of oppression.
