Sarojini Naidu

Indian Independence Activist and Poet

Born: February 13, 1879

Died: March 2, 1949

Sarojini Naidu was a leading figure in the Indian independence movement and a renowned poet.

Early Life

Sarojini was born in Hyderabad, India.

She was educated in India and England.

Freedom Movement

Sarojini was a key figure in the Indian National Congress.

She was the first woman to be appointed President of the Indian National Congress.
